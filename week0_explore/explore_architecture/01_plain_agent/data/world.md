# World Map (Midgaard)

## Known Rooms

- **Market Square** (start/hub): exits n(Temple Square) e(Main Street) s(Common Square) w(Main Street)

### East of Market (Main Street)
- Main Street (near market): n=General Store, s=Pet Shop, continues e/w
- Main Street (further east): n=Weapon Shop, s=Guild of Swordsmen, continues e/w
- Weapon Shop: dead end (exit s only)
- Main Street (near gate): s=Water Shop (Ye Olde Water Shoppe), continues e/w
- Inside East Gate of Midgaard: s=Water Shop, exits e/s/w
- Outside East Gate of Midgaard: plains to the east, exits e/w

### North of Market (Temple area)
- Temple Square: w=Clerics' Guild entrance, e=Grunting Boar Inn, exits n/e/s/w
  - has fountain, oozing blob, cityguard
- Temple of Midgaard (n of Temple Square): w=Reading Room, e=Donation Room, exits n/e/s/w/d
  - has ATM
- Reading Room (w of Temple): teleporter device, bulletin board, a saleswoman with gadgets, exit e only
- Midgaard Donation Room (e of Temple): free starter equipment pile, exit w only
- By The Temple Altar (n of Temple): statue of Odin, exits n/s
- Behind The Temple Altar: dirt path, exits n/s
- The Great Field of Midgaard: countryside, exits n/s
- Entrance to Clerics' Guild (w of Temple Square): n=bar (GUARDED - knight templar blocks entry), e=Temple Square, has ATM

- Eastern End of Poor Alley (w of Common Sq): s=Grubby Inn, w=continues, e=Common Square
- Poor Alley (further west): dead-end-ish, city wall to west, beggar NPC, exits e/w only
- Dark Alley (e of Common Sq): s=Guild of Thieves, exits e/s/w, mercenary NPC
- Dark Alley At The Levee (further east): s=the levee, exits e/w/s — NOT YET EXPLORED FURTHER
